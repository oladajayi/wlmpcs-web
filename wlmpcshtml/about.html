WAEC LOKOJA MULTI-PURPOSE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETY LTD.

We are a WAEC in-house Cooperative Society located at WAEC Office, Hassan Usman Katsina Road, Zone 8, P.M.B. 1050. Lokoja, Kogi State.

Our aims and objectives include to:

  1. Encourage savings among members and establish a fund from which members can be granted loans at an agreed percentage of interest.
  2. Procure consumer goods for sale to members at reasonable prices
  3. Encourage joint ventures with other sisters' societies in WAEC, Lokoja, and Nigeria.
  4. Encourage the well-being of members.
