TERMS AND CONDITIONS

MEMBERSHIP

Membership shall be opened to:

  1. All interested WAEC, Lokoja staff.
  2. Any interested staff of WAEC in Nigeria.
  3. Spy police unit of WAEC Lokoja and other WAEC offices in Nigeria.
  4. All interested retired WAEC staff.

QUALIFICATION FOR MEMBERSHIP

  1. Indication of interest on written application through the Secretary by the intending member.
  2. Member must be of good character.

MEMBERSHIP REGISTRATION FEE

SECTION (A)

A member shall be required to pay a non-refundable membership registration fee of N500.00 (five hundred naira) only. This amount shall be reviewed from time to time.

SECTION (B)

MEMBERSHIP MONTHLY SUBSCRIPTION A member shall be required to pay a minimum monthly subscription of Nl,000.00 (one thousand naira) only. This amount shall be reviewed from time to time.

SECTION (C)

INTEREST ON LOANS The Society shall operate the following loans;

  1. NORMALLOAN Members shall be qualified to take twice their total subscription, less any outstanding indebtedness. Applicant shall have, however, contributed to the Society for at least six (6) months. Otherwise, section (c)(2) applies. Applicants MUST also meet the conditions set out in section 17. (a). INTEREST ON NORMAL LOAN REFUNDABLE Between 1-18 months = 5% Between 19-24 months = 7%.
