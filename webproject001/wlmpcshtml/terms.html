TERMS AND CONDITIONS

SECTION 1: AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The aims and objectives of the Society shall include to:

  1. Encourage savings among members and to establish a fund from which members can be granted loans at an agreed percentage of interest.
  2. Procure consumer goods for sale to members at reasonable prices.
  3. Encourage joint venture with other sisters' societies in WAEC, Lokoja and Nigeria.
  4. Encourage the well being of members.

MEMBERSHIP

Membership shall be opened to:

  1. All interested WAEC, Lokoja staff
  2. Any interested staff of WAEC in Nigeria
  3. Spy police unit of WAEC Lokoja and other WAEC offices in Nigeria.

SECTION 2 (A): QUALIFICATION FOR MEMBERSHIP

  1. Indication of interest on written application through the Secretary by the intending member.
  2. Payment of mandatory registration fee of N500.00 (five hundred naira) only. This shall be subject to review from time to time.
  3. Member must be of good character.

SECTION 3 (A): MEMBERSHIP REGISTRATION FEE

A member shall be required to pay a non-refundable membership registration fee of N500.00 (five hundred naira) only. This amount shall be reviewed from time to time.

SECTION 3 (B): MEMBERSHIP MONTHLY SUBSCRIPTION

A member shall be required to pay a minimum monthly subscription of N1,000.00 (one thousand naira) only. This amount shall be reviewed from time to time.

SECTION 3 (C): INTEREST ON LOANS

1. Normal Loan

Member shall be qualified to take twice their total subscription, less any outstanding indebtedness. Applicant must have contributed for at least six (6) months. Otherwise section 14 (c)(2) applies.

Interest Rates on Normal Loan:

  • Between 1-18 months = 5%
  • Between 19-24 months = 7%

2. Excess Loan

Member may ask for an amount greater than twice contributions. Such loan attracts 10% interest.

3. Discounted Loan

Special loan to be redeemed from allowances (rent advance, leave allowance, medical, education, premium, etc.).

Interest Rates:

  • 0-6 Months = 7.5%
  • 7-9 Months = 10%
  • 10-12 Months = 12%

4. Determination of Interest

Interest on loans shall be determined by simple interest rate method.

SECTION 3 (D): PROFITS FROM INVESTMENT AND BANK DEPOSIT

Includes investment in Federal Government Treasury bills and bank deposit interest.

SECTION 3 (E): GRANTS AND DONATIONS

Includes voluntary donations from members and/or non-members in appreciation of the Society.

SECTION 4: OUSTER CLAUSES

Special loan facility ONLY for staff whose pay point is WAEC Lokoja. Others must provide two guarantors from WAEC Lokoja.

SECTION 5: CONDITIONS FOR GRANTING LOANS

  1. Applicant shall collect and complete a loan application form obtained from the Secretary at a fee of N100.00.
  2. Upon Executive Committee approval, applicant shall complete a loan bond form.
  3. Applicant shall provide two guarantors who MUST be members of the Society.
  4. Approved loan shall be paid via cheque or e-payment.
  5. In case of default, guarantors shall pay 50/50 of the unsettled debt.
  6. Failure to liquidate renegotiated loans shall attract a minimum of 15% interest; if unpaid after three months, guarantors are held liable.

SECTION 6: LIABILITY OF MEMBER

A member shall be liable to:

  1. Unpaid subscriptions and debts owed to the Society.
  2. Estate of deceased member up to the unpaid debt of the deceased.

SECTION 7: DISPENSATION OF GENERATED INTEREST

  1. Not less than 73% of total income shall be shared as dividend among members.
  2. Not more than 25% shall be charged as administrative expenditure.
  3. 2% shall be reserved for future investment.

SECTION 8: CESSATION OF MEMBERSHIP

A staff ceases to be a member by:

  • Retirement
  • Withdrawal
  • Termination
  • Transfer (optional)
  • Dismissal
  • Voluntary withdrawal
  • Death
  • Permanent insanity
  • Failure to hold one full share within three years of admission

SECTION 10: WITHDRAWAL FROM SAVINGS ACCOUNT

Conditions:

  • Permitted once per year
  • Maximum of N100,000.00
  • 3% interest charge applies
  • No standing loan at time of withdrawal

SECTION 11: SHARE CAPITAL

All members shall subscribe to a minimum of N50,000.00, payable in 24 months installments. Subject to review.

SECTION 12: STATISTICAL DATA TO BE DISCLOSED

  1. Percentage of total share on total income
  2. Total dividend payable on shares
  3. Percentage of total savings on total income
  4. Total interest payable on savings
  5. Share per naira
  6. Total amount payable on share
  7. Interest per naira
  8. Total amount payable on savings
  9. Percentage of expenditure to total income
  10. Percentage of net surplus to total income
  11. Percentage of committee bonus to total income

SECTION 13: SCHEDULES IN FINANCIAL STATEMENT

  • Members savings and loan schedule
  • Dividend/Interest schedule
  • Monthly loan and interest schedule

SECTION 14: NOMINEES/NEXT OF KIN

Members shall nominate one or two persons as beneficiaries. Nominees may be changed in writing anytime.

SECTION 15: EXPULSION OF MEMBER

A member may be expelled for:

  • Failure to save for three months
  • Failure to pay share installments or debts
  • Conviction on criminal offence involving dishonesty or misconduct

SECTION 16: WITHDRAWAL OF MEMBERSHIP

  1. Give at least three months notice in writing
  2. Receive all contributions less indebtedness and charges
  3. Dividend/interest deferred to the next AGM
